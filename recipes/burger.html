Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and top with parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
